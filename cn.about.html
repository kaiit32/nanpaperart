关于我们

阿南老师

PROFILE:
- Reputed Chinese artist of original paper art, paper mold, and origami.
- Founder of Nan Paper Art Studio (2012) and Nan Refined Paper Art Training Institute.
- Senior Lecturer in the School of Art, Fudan University, Shanghai
- First Chinese artist to bring paper arts into an international fashion show - Perrier-Jouët
- Official window display paper art designer for Mercedes-Benz, Porsche China,H&M, ZARA, C&A
- TV set paper art designer for CCTV, Hunan Satellite TV, Jiangsu Satellite TV, Zhejiang Satellite TV
- Fashion show collaboration as a paper designer for Dior, Lancôme, Estée Lauder, Hermès




Our Team

Established in 2012, Nan Paper Art has made its mark in China as a high-end paper design brand. Synonymous with the most intuitive, Nan Paper Art uses an expert eye to find the perfect combination of space, color and aesthetics, continuously pushing the boundaries of existing materials and techniques. The company’s aim is the ultimate pursuit of beauty. The creations are concerned with narrative, they seek to lead you into a story or environment and invite you to be moved by this. Nan Paper Art not only represents the highest level of domestic paper design, but also is leading the wave of Chinese paper production and design. Over the past six years, Nan Paper Art has worked with hundreds of companies and organisations on both small projects and big creations. It has become the top paper design company for many internationally renowned brands both in China and worldwide. Its vast scope of services include top luxury weddings, window display, space decoration design, high-end public relations activities and TV show stage sets.
